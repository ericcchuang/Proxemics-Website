Participants

Each participant had their own unique traits, both as stated through interviews, and revealed through their poster placing process. Explore below!

Participant 1

Traits:

  • Moderately involved in niche clubs
  • Commonly found in Weston Hall (School of Architecture and Design)
  • Came in with a design plan
  • Tried to cover up the white space on some posters
  • Prioritized well-designed posters

Participant 2

Traits:

  • Very involved in a larger club
  • Has an informed friend group
  • Commonly found in Campus Center
  • Prioritized academic and event posters
  • Hesitant to overlap, prefered completely covering
  • Personal preference played a strong part in decisions

Participant 3

Traits:

  • Generally uninvolved
  • Commonly found in their dorm room
  • Prioritized text over images
  • Created more space by overhanging the bottom and top

Participant 4

Traits:

  • Participates in club activities daily
  • Has an informed friend group
  • Commonly found in Central King Building
  • Attempted to be unbiased in terms of content
  • Did not sort through posters before placing
  • De-prioritized more permanent fixtures
